**Physics Poem**

Oh, Physics, thou art a wondrous thing,
A realm of knowledge where I love to sing.
Thy laws govern all that we see and know,
From the tiniest atom to the celestial show.

Thy principles are elegant and grand,
A symphony of forces, I understand.
Gravity binds us to the Earth's embrace,
While electromagnetism lights up space.

Momentum and energy, forever entwined,
In a cosmic dance, forever defined.
Waves and particles, a duality so strange,
Unveiling the secrets of nature's range.

Oh, Physics, thou art a boundless sea,
A source of wonder and discovery for me.
I delve into thy depths with eager delight,
Seeking knowledge that will forever ignite.
